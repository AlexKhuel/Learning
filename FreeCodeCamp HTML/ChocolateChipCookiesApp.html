Chocolate Chip Cookies

This recipe will make 36 medium size chocolate chip cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup salted butter softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup light brown sugar packed
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • Â½ teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips (12 oz)

Instructions

  1. Creams together butter and sugars
  2. Add eggs & vanilla and beat
  3. Add dry ingredients and stir until just combined
  4. Add chocolate chips
  5. Portion using a cooking scoop
  6. Place 2 inches apart and bake at 375 for 8 minutes
